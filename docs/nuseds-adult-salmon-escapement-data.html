New Salmon Escapement Database System (NuSEDS)

NuSEDS is the Pacific Regionâ€™s centralized Oracle database that holds adult salmon escapement data. About 10,000 salmon spawning sites in DFO Pacific Region have been observed 0 to 10 times per year for 6 species for nearly 100 years (poorly before 1948). Data is aggregated within year; the raw data is largely on paper.
